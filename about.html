About Us

Introduction: At NSC (Nahom Sister Company), our mission is clear and resolute: to spearhead the digital transformation of Africa. We recognize the immense potential and untapped opportunities that technology can bring to this diverse and dynamic continent. With unwavering dedication, NSC is committed to being a catalyst for progress and innovation across Africa.

Our Mission in Action: 1.Digital Infrastructure Development: NSC is actively involved in building robust digital infrastructure across Africa. We are investing in high-speed internet connectivity, data centers, and telecommunications networks to bridge the digital divide and ensure that every corner of the continent is digitally connected. 2.Tech Education and Skill Development: To empower the African workforce, NSC is passionate about education and skill development. We collaborate with educational institutions, organizations, and governments to provide training and resources that equip individuals with the skills needed for the digital age. 3.Entrepreneurship and Innovation: NSC encourages entrepreneurship and innovation through various initiatives. We support startups, incubators, and accelerators, providing resources, mentorship, and funding to nurture local innovation and drive economic growth. 4.E-Government Solutions: We partner with governments to create efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric e-government solutions. Our aim is to streamline public services, enhance governance, and make government more accessible to the people. 5. Digital Inclusion: NSC is committed to ensuring that no one is left behind in the digital revolution. We work on projects that promote digital literacy, accessibility, and affordability, making technology accessible to all segments of society.

Conclusion: NSC's mission to digitalize Africa is not just an ambition; it's a pledge to transform lives and empower communities. We invite you to join us on this transformative journey as we bring the benefits of technology to Africa's people, businesses, and governments.
